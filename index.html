About Me

Hey, thanks for visiting my portfolio page! My name is Alex Perkins and I'm a part-time student through the University of Washington's Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp.


My professional interests include cloud computing, Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS), UX/UI, and sales engineering/solutions architecture. Through the combination of my current work as a Technical Account Manager at SkyKick, as well as this program, I'm fortunate to spend a large amount of time honing and refining these interests!


When I'm not at work or studying, I can be found exploring some of the many great National and State Parks the Pacific Northwest has to offer, playing guitar, reading, spending time with my beautiful wife Waverly, and hanging out with our two pet bunnies, Sage and Juniper.
